About Us

Our capital is unlevered, untethered, and uncorrupted — just like the systems we’re building.

Our Mission

Mawji Holdings exists to fund and build the companies that take back control. We exist to back rebels, create unkillable tech, and protect the sovereignty of individuals — online and off.

Our Vision

We want to build a world where technology serves people — not corporations or governments. We aim to lead the global shift toward digital sovereignty, economic independence, and encrypted freedom — and ensure the future doesn’t look like 1984.

How We Work?

Fund

Uncorrupted capital. We don’t raise from outsiders. We deploy our own money. No LPs. No compromises.

Venture Studio

Where we build with exceptional founders. This is our engine for developing technology aligned with our mission. If we can’t find it in the world, we build it ourselves.

Spin-Outs

When something earns its teeth, we spin it out as an independent company. Each spinout is fully resourced, sovereign, and structured with its own cap table, officers, and path to scale.

Treasury

Managed with intent. We operate with private capital across multiple asset classes and technologies — structured for long-term control, privacy, and operational resilience. We will weather any storm or oppressive government.
